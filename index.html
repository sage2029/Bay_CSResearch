The Impact of Artificial Intelligence on Education in Myanmar (Burma)
Aung Ko Ko Oo, Nyi Nyi Min Khant
ResearchGate Preprint Paper.2025
Artificial Intelligence has revolutionized the world of education by improving the efficiency of teaching, personalization, and accessibility. In the case of countries like Myanmar, whose educational systems are still developing, AI can play a significant role in reducing barriers to education and improving educational achievements. In this paper, the effects of Artificial Intelligence on the educational system in Myanmar will be investigated. The role of AI in educational systems and how AI affects students and teachers will also be examined. This paper uses the method of desk-based studies and case studies that involve AI-based educational systems in the South Asian region. The results of this particular study suggest that if the government and the private sectors can manage the situation aptly, AI can play a significant role in personalization techniques and the development of digital literacy.
